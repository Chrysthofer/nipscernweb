Calorimeter Geometry Viewer Web

CGV-WEB is the online evolution of CaloGeoView. It renders CERN calorimeter events in 3D, colour-coding each detector cell by energy deposit, giving physicists a powerful visual tool for understanding collision data — accessible worldwide, no installation required.

CGV-WEB is used in lectures and outreach activities at UFJF and CERN to help students understand how the ATLAS detector registers particle collision events.

Origin: CaloGeoView

CGV-WEB is the online evolution of CaloGeoView, a desktop visualisation tool originally developed as part of the PhD thesis of Dr. Luciano Manhães de Andrade Filho at UFJF. The original CaloGeoView ran as a native application; CGV-WEB rebuilds the core in Rust/WebAssembly to make it accessible to anyone with a modern web browser — researchers, students, and the public alike — at nipscern.com/projects/cgv.
